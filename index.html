About Our Company

[BEGCS (Pty) Ltd] - Bidecal Electronics and Gadgets Cleaning Services

At BEGCS, we specialize in the cleaning and disinfection of laptops, computers, keyboards, and other electronic gadgets. Our mission is to enhance public health and device longevity by providing targeted cleaning solutions and creating awareness about the importance of maintaining hygiene for electronic devices.

Our Vision

We aim to create a healthier environment by reducing the spread of germs and bacteria through contaminated devices, which are often overlooked in hygiene routines. By ensuring regular disinfection, we contribute to good health and well-being (SDG 3) while also prolonging the lifespan and economic value of electronic gadgets.
